Flexible Feedback was created as a winter break project by middle-school teacher Christine Witcher. There are small operating costs associated with running and developing this site. Your donations are appreciated!


Narrative feedback is incredibly important in the classroom. Research tells us that grades without feedback have very limited worth, but we also know that substantial feedback takes hours to write. Flexible Feedback is a web-based tool that makes feedback writing more efficient.


What about student data security?

Flexible Feedback is a "client-side" application. This means that there is no server and nothing you enter into the screen is passed through the internet. It's sort of like opening a program on your computer, but the program opens in your browser. In summary, student data stays on your computer and doesn't go anywhere else, nor can we see what you enter.
